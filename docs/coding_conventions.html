Coding conventions

Special characters

The conventions for rendering special characters are adopted from the Helsinki Corpus.
Feature Edition PCEEC2
superscript wch w=ch=
abbreviation promocõns promoco~ns
accents Trenyté Trenyte` (acute/grave/circumflex are not differentiated)
thorn (upper case) Þ +T
thorn (lower case) þ +t
yogh (upper case) 3orke +Gorke
yogh (lower case) 3e +ge
pound sign £4 +L4

Text markup

The conventions for indicating editorial comments and other types of text markup are the same as used in the Penn Parsed Corpora of Historical English.
